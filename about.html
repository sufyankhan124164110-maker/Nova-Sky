About Nova Sky

Nova Sky is an independent astronomy project created by Sufyan Khan. It started out of a simple passion for space and the night sky, and a wish to make real astronomical data — satellite passes, planet positions, star maps — easy and free for anyone to explore, without needing specialized equipment or software.

Everything on this site runs live: the International Space Station's real position and upcoming visible passes, the current positions of the planets and Moon from wherever you're standing, and a simulated live sky map built from real star catalog data and orbital mechanics. The goal is simple — to help curious people connect with the sky above them, even through clouds, city lights, or daylight.

Who's behind it

Nova Sky is built and maintained by Sufyan Khan as a personal passion project. It's a work in progress, with new features added over time.
