Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Nova Sky uses and how it's handled. Nova Sky is operated by Sufyan Khan.

Location Data

Nova Sky asks for your device's location to show satellite passes, planet positions, and the star map relevant to where you are. This location is used only in your browser, in real time, to perform calculations. It is not stored, logged, or sent to any server owned by Nova Sky.

Camera & Motion Sensors

The AR Sky feature requests camera access and device orientation (compass/tilt) sensors to overlay sky data on your live camera view. This video feed is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Nova Sky fetches publicly available data from third-party APIs to power certain features, including Where The ISS At (wheretheiss.at) and the ISS Pass API (polluxlabs.io) for satellite data, and the Astronomy Engine library for planetary calculations. These services may receive your approximate coordinates as part of the request in order to return relevant results. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle data.

Advertising

Nova Sky may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and manage ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com.

Cookies

This site may use cookies placed by third-party advertising or analytics services. Nova Sky itself does not set cookies to track individual users.
